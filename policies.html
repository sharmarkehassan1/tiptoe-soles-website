Store Policies

These policies explain how we handle exchanges, refunds and privacy in a clear and customer-friendly way.

Returns and Exchanges

  • Items may be exchanged within 30 days of purchase with proof of purchase.
  • Shoes should be unworn, in original condition and in their original box.
  • If a product is faulty, we will work with you to arrange an appropriate remedy.

Delivery Policy

  • Orders are processed as quickly as possible after payment is confirmed.
  • Estimated delivery times may vary depending on location and stock availability.
  • Customers will be contacted if there is a delay with their order.

Privacy

  • Customer details are used only for completing purchases and providing service.
  • We do not share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Contact details may be used to provide updates about your order or product enquiries.
