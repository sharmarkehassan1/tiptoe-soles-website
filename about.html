About TipToe Soles

TipToe Soles is dedicated to helping customers find footwear that supports their lifestyle without sacrificing style or comfort.

Our Story

TipToe Soles started as a small local retailer with a simple goal: to make comfortable shoes easier to find.

Today, we offer a carefully selected range suitable for work, casual outings and everyday wear.

What We Offer

  • Comfortable and supportive footwear for everyday use
  • Styles suitable for both casual and formal occasions
  • Friendly, personalised service to help you find the right fit
  • A focus on long-lasting materials and quality construction

Why Shop With Us?

  • Modern and easy-to-navigate website design
  • Helpful staff who understand foot comfort and support
  • Carefully chosen brands and models
  • Focus on both comfort and style
